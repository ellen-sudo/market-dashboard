📋 How to connect your Google Sheet (one-time setup)
  1. Open your Google Sheet
  2. Click File → Share → Publish to web
  3. Left dropdown: choose "Form Responses 1"
  4. Right dropdown: choose "Comma-separated values (.csv)"
  5. Click Publish and copy the URL
  6. Paste it above and click Load Data — from now on, just fill out your form each month and the dashboard updates automatically ✅
